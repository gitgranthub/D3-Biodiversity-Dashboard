The Study and Data

In the dashboard below, you will see the term OTU used and a value given to it. OTU, "Operational Taxonomic Unit", is simply the group of organisms currently being studied. The dataset reveals that a small handful of microbial species (also called operational taxonomic units, or OTUs, in the study) were present in more than 70% of people, while the rest were relatively rare.
